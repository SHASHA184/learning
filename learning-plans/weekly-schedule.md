## Templates for Different Schedules

### Busy Professional (5-7 hours/week)
- **Monday**: 1 hour - theory
- **Tuesday**: 30 min - practice
- **Wednesday**: 30 min - review
- **Thursday**: 1 hour - implementation
- **Friday**: 30 min - advanced topics
- **Weekend**: 2-3 hours - projects

### Intensive Learning (15-20 hours/week)
- **Monday**: 3 hours - deep learning
- **Tuesday**: 2 hours - practice
- **Wednesday**: 2 hours - implementation
- **Thursday**: 3 hours - projects
- **Friday**: 2 hours - advanced topics
- **Weekend**: 4-6 hours - integration

### Casual Learning (3-5 hours/week)
- **Monday**: 1 hour - theory
- **Wednesday**: 1 hour - practice
- **Friday**: 1 hour - implementation
- **Weekend**: 1-2 hours - review/projects

## Customization Notes

**Adapt this template based on:**
- Your available time slots
- Your learning style preferences
- The complexity of your chosen topics
- Your current skill level
- External commitments and deadlines

**Tips for Success:**
- Be realistic with time estimates
- Include buffer time for difficult topics
- Mix theory with practical application
- Regular review prevents forgetting
- Adjust schedule based on what works
- Track both time and quality of learning
